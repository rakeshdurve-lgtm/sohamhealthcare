Diagnostic & Imaging services

On-site lab, MRI, CT scan, and X-ray with same-day reports. Following services are availabel: Digital X-Ray, CT Scan (Computed Tomography), MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging), Ultrasound (Sonography), ECG, and Blood test.

Panchakarma

Panchakarma is a comprehensive Ayurvedic detoxification and rejuvenation program. The name translates to "Five Actions" (Panch = Five, Karma = Actions), referring to the five primary procedures used to cleanse the body of deep-seated toxins and restore balance to the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, and Kapha)
